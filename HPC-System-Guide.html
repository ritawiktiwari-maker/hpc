System Overview

The Hygienic Pest Control system is a full-stack platform consisting of three interconnected modules:

Public Website

Customer-facing site showcasing services, company info, testimonials, and contact forms. Designed to generate leads and build trust.

CRM Admin Panel

Internal management system for customers, employees, inventory, job assignments, leads, reports, and website content.

Employee Portal

Dedicated portal for field staff to view assigned jobs, manage stock-in-hand, complete services, and request stock returns.

Customer Management

CRM

How to Use

Navigate to Customers
Click "Customers" in the sidebar to view the customer list.
Add a Customer
Click "Add Customer" button. Fill in Name, Address, Contact Number, Email, Service Type, and Frequency. Click Save.
Search & Filter
Use the search bar to find customers by name or contact number.
Edit or Delete
Click the action menu on any customer row to edit details, view service history, toggle active status, or delete.
View Service History
Click "Service History" to see all completed jobs for a customer, including dates, services, and employees involved.
Import / Export
Use "Export" to download customer data as Excel. Use "Import" to bulk-add customers from an Excel file.

Stock & Inventory Management

CRM

7.1 Current Stock Tab

Add a Product
Click "Add Product". Enter product name, category (Chemical / Machine), unit (Litres, ML, KG, MG, Pieces), quantity, supplier name, purchase date, and remarks. A unique Product ID is auto-generated (PRD0001).
Monitor Stock Levels
View real-time quantities. Color-coded indicators: In Stock Low (≤10) Out of Stock
Restock
Click "Restock" on a product to add quantity. The new amount is added to the existing stock and a PURCHASE transaction is logged.
Export to Excel
Click "Export" to download the full inventory as an Excel spreadsheet.

7.2 Stock History Tab

A complete audit trail of all stock movements:

Transaction TypeDescriptionQuantity
PURCHASENew stock added to inventory+ (increase)
ASSIGN_TO_EMPLOYEEStock given to employee for a job− (decrease)
RETURN_FROM_EMPLOYEEUnused stock returned by employee+ (increase)
USED_IN_VISITStock consumed during a service− (decrease)

7.3 Stock Return Approvals

Navigate to Stock Approvals in the sidebar. When employees request to return stock:

  • View pending return requests with employee name, products, and quantities
  • Approve: Stock returned to main inventory, employee stock reduced
  • Reject: Stock remains with employee, request marked as rejected

Job Assignment & Tracking

CRM

Creating a New Job Assignment

Navigate to Jobs → New Assignment
Click "Jobs" in the sidebar, then select the "New Assignment" tab.
Fill in Job Details
Enter bill number, select a customer from the dropdown, choose an active employee, set the job date, select service type, and optionally set the next service date.
Assign Products
Select products from inventory and specify quantities. The system validates available stock. These products will be assigned to the employee's stock-in-hand.
Enter Bill Amount & Remarks
Set the billing amount and add any special instructions or notes.
Submit Assignment
On submission: the job is created as PENDING, products are deducted from main stock and added to the employee's stock, and stock transactions are logged.

Job Lifecycle

Assigned (PENDING)
→
Employee Performs Service
→
Completed

Job History Tab

View all jobs with: bill number, customer name, employee, service type, date, status, products used, and remarks. Sorted chronologically.

Status Legend

PENDING — Job assigned, awaiting completion by employee

COMPLETED — Service finished, products consumed and logged

Employee Portal & Workflows

Employee

12.1 Employee Login

Employees access their portal at /employee/login. Login with Employee ID (e.g., EMP0001) and password set by admin.

12.2 Employee Dashboard

Pending Jobs Count — Jobs awaiting completion
Completed Jobs Count — Finished services
Stock in Hand — Products currently assigned

12.3 Completing a Job

View Pending Jobs
The "Pending Jobs" tab shows all assigned jobs with customer name, bill number, service type, and assigned products list.
Click "Complete Job"
Opens a dialog where the employee enters the actual quantity of each product used during the service.
Confirm Completion
System updates: job status → COMPLETED, employee stock reduced by amount used, USED_IN_VISIT transaction logged, completion date recorded.

12.4 Returning Unused Stock

Click "Return Stock"
From the "My Stock in Hand" section, click the return button.
Select Products & Quantities
Choose which products to return and enter the quantities.
Submit Return Request
Request goes to admin for approval. Status: PENDING → APPROVED / REJECTED.
Important Note

Employees can only see their own jobs and stock. They cannot access other employees' data, customer details, or admin functions.
